# WANDILE — A Zambian Beat 'Em Up

A Double Dragon–style arcade brawler made for Wandile. The Crocodile Gang has
stolen his puppy **Loki**, and Wandile fights across Zambia to get him back.

## How to play

Just open `index.html` in any browser — no install, no internet needed.
On a phone or tablet, on-screen touch controls appear automatically.

| Action  | Keys              |
|---------|-------------------|
| Move    | Arrow keys / WASD |
| Punch   | J or Z            |
| Kick    | K or X            |
| Jump    | Space (punch/kick in the air for a jump kick) |
| Eagle Strike | L or C (needs at least half the orange meter) |
| Pause   | P                 |
| Mute    | M                 |
| Fullscreen | F              |
| Start / Continue | Enter    |

## Tips

- Punch three times in a row for a combo finisher that knocks goons down.
- Break wooden crates — they hide food (vitumbuwa, maheu, nshima) that
  restores health, and weapons like sticks, pot lids, and a football.
  Walk over a new weapon to swap; your old one is set down behind you.
- Landing hits fills the orange meter. When it flashes, press L and a
  Zambian fish eagle sweeps the whole screen, flattening every enemy.
- Bosses are armored while charging — dodge up or down, then strike when
  they're dizzy. Each boss has a taunt and its own battle theme.
- K skips the story on replays. The game auto-pauses if you switch windows.

## The journey

1. **Lusaka** — streets & Soweto Market · boss: Mad Mongoose
2. **Copperbelt** — the Kitwe mine · boss: The Driller
3. **The Bush Road** — village at sunset · boss: Hyena
4. **Victoria Falls** — Mosi-oa-Tunya · boss: Captain Ng'wena
